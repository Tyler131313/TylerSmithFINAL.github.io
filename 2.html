We train our horses around the lake, crossing the creek, and around the ocean

In the show ring, in a parade, riding inside, riding in a pack, riding through waterfalls, and riding 10,000+ feet

We subscribe to the philosophy and practice of wet saddle blankets, and train our horses to:

flex and bend at the poal, back on light rein, neck rein, forehand turn, pivot, side pass, center on correct leads, cross any obstacle, stand tied with patience, respect its handler/rider, and tolerate dogs, traffic, gun fire, and other frightening experiences.

We produce barefoot and keg and lite shod pleasure horses with extensive traveling, camping and trail experience ready for you to ride anywhere.

Our staff has over 114 years of combined experience

Our staff has been raising, buying, selling, training, competing, and loving horses since 1983. We have sold horses all across the US, Canada, and even Israel. We love horses and sharing our love with others!
